Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 July 2026

Production site: thehybridengine1.netlify.app

THE Hybrid System is a local-first training app for building, scheduling and logging workouts. This notice explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information stored on your device

The current app stores workout templates, scheduled sessions, completed sessions, notes, preferences and backups in your browser on your device. This local data is not sent to us by the static app. You can export or remove it from the app's Settings area or by clearing the app's browser data.

Optional WHOOP and Strava connections

The app includes optional WHOOP and Strava connection controls when deployed with the accompanying server-side functions. If you choose to connect one of these services, the app requests the permissions you approve and uses the resulting data for recovery or activity context and conditioning load.

Integration credentials and access tokens are handled by server-side infrastructure, not stored in the browser or included in the downloadable app package. The connector stores encrypted tokens and synced provider records in its server-side Netlify storage while the integration is enabled. Disconnecting removes the stored token and synced record from the app store; you can also revoke access through the relevant provider.

How information is used

  • To provide workout building, scheduling, logging and history features.
  • To calculate and display training and conditioning information you ask the app to show.
  • To maintain backups or integration syncs that you explicitly enable.

We do not sell personal information or use it for advertising.

Sharing and retention

Local workout data stays on your device unless you export it or explicitly enable an integration. If an integration is enabled, provider data is retained in server-side storage for the operation of that feature and can be removed by disconnecting the service or requesting deletion.

Security

Connection credentials must remain server-side and should never be placed in the app, a backup, a ZIP package or a public repository. The production site at thehybridengine1.netlify.app is served over HTTPS.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, use the contact email associated with the developer application for this site.
